Every website is made of sections that display content and help orient users. Sections can be designd in a variety of ways and combined with other sections to create a unique page. The Starter has 6 sections and a wide variety of layouts to mix and match for the perfect look.

Not sure what a website section is? Check out this reference sheet for a guided tour of each section type.

The navigation bar usually appears at the top of the page, though some websites are intentionally designed with it at the bottom.

Navigation bars typically display a logo or brand name, along with a few links to different parts of the website. The links may collapse when viewed on a mobile, but remain accessible through a menu icon.

The masthead is usually found at the top of a page, displaying an image, logo, or slogan. Mastheads range in size and design, with the primary goal being to grab attention.
